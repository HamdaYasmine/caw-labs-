The ongoing situation in Gaza since October 7, 2023, has resulted in catastrophic humanitarian consequences and widespread destruction.

Humanitarian Impact Statistics:

Martyrs: Over 35,000 Palestinians killed

Children: More than 14,000 children martyred

Women: Over 9,000 women martyred

Injured: Approximately 79,000 people injured

Displaced: About 1.9 million people displaced (85% of population)

The scale of destruction in Gaza is unprecedented, with entire neighborhoods reduced to rubble and critical infrastructure completely demolished.

Infrastructure Damage:

Housing Units: Over 70% of homes damaged or destroyed

Hospitals: Majority of hospitals non-functional

Schools: More than 80% of schools damaged

Water Systems: Most water and sanitation systems destroyed

Economic Loss: Estimated $18.5 billion in damage
